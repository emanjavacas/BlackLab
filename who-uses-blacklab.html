Who is using BlackLab?

  • The Institute of Dutch Lexicology (INL) is developing BlackLab and uses it for their corpus search applications, such as Letters as loot, Corpus Gysseling and the Corpus Hedendaags Nederlands (CLARIN login required), a 500M+ token corpus of contemporary Dutch.
  • University of Tilburg (OpenSonar, a Dutch corpus)
  • Meertens Institute (Fesli, a [bilingual] children’s language corpus)
  • Phil Burns of Northwestern University has created an experimental corpus search site for the MorphAdorner project that’s powered by BlackLab. Martin Mueller (Professor of English & Classics) has written about his experiences with the application.- If you’re using BlackLab, let me know, I’d like to mention your project here!
  • Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (IKE, a knowledge extraction tool)
  • Radboud Universiteit (Basilex, a corpus of text written for children, OpenChechen, a Chechen corpus).
  • VIVA, Virtuele Instituut Vir Afrikaans (Korpusportaal, an Afrikaans corpus (paid access only))
